03  Music and Audio Background

The Full
Picture.

The name DAW Profit is more than a stage identity — DAW are his initials: Daniel Andrew Ward. It's a name that coincides perfectly with a career built inside a DAW (Digital Audio Workstation), where every beat, arrangement, mix, and vocal in his catalog has been crafted entirely from scratch.

Daniel started as a rapper and producer in parallel — two disciplines that rarely develop together. That dual foundation gave him complete creative control from the first idea to the final master. He's a multi-instrumentalist (guitar, drums, keys), a trained studio engineer, and a live performer tested on real stages with real crowds.

He cut his teeth with One Light Records and Mogul Minds Network, interned at Stonecutter Studios, and continues to develop as a drummer and vocalist for indie rock outfit SheepClouds — a second, fully separate creative lane that deepens his musicianship without diluting the DAW Profit brand. Today Profit is a fully independent creative entity: writing, producing, mixing, performing, and engineering across Chicago and the Midwest.
